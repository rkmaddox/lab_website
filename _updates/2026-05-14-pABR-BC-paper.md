Our paper with the [Polonenko Lab](https://sites.google.com/umn.edu/pololab) comparing bone and air conduction response in the pABR has been published in [_Ear and Hearing_](https://doi.org/10.1097/AUD.0000000000001833). The study is a proof of concept in adults with normal hearing that shows that bone and air conduction provide similar response waveforms for the pABR and that transducer artifact does not present any problems. It also provides dB nHL norms for pABR stimuli with the bone vibrator.
